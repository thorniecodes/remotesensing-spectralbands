# Sources & References

The spectral band data, wavelength ranges, sensor specifications, and operational status information presented in the [Remote Sensing Band Guide]({{ '/' | relative_url }}) were compiled exclusively from primary sources — official space agency documentation, government technical specifications, and peer-reviewed scientific publications. No secondary or aggregated databases were used.

Each source below corresponds directly to data cited in the band table. Where a band row carries a source pill (USGS, ESA, NASA, JAXA, DLR, or ASI), the full citation for that agency can be found here. Citations are formatted in APA 7th edition style.

**A note on dates:** Sources marked *n.d.* (no date) are government or agency specification pages that do not display a visible publication or last-updated date — this is standard practice for institutional web documentation. APA 7th edition instructs the use of *n.d.* in these cases. Retrieval dates are included for all web sources, as page content may be updated over time. Where a specific publication date was known — such as an official advisory document or a mission launch announcement — that date is used instead.
